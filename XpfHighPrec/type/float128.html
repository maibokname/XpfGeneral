Float128

type, public :: Float128

Float128 is a derived type representing a signed quadruple-precision floating-point number. Internally, it consists of a logical variable representing its sign, two 64-bit integers representing its 128-bit significand, and a 32-bit integer representing its (biased) exponent. Its value ranges from approximately 6.67283E-646457032 to 1.76161E+646456993.
